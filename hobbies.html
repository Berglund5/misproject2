Why These Hobbies Are Meaningful to Me

Golf is a sport that I truly enjoy because of how much of a mental game it is, there is nobody else to blame but yourself. I also love how much of a scenic game it is, the views at a lot of courses are surreal and awesome. I got into golf mainly because of my Grandfather, but I have just found a true love for the game. I go as much as I can whether that is with my Dad, Bestfriend, Girlfriend or even random people, no matter who it is with it is always a good time! As for watching sports I have really loved professional sports for a long time. I have been watching the Vikings every weekend with my family for as long as I can remember. One of the biggest Vikings games I can remember was the 2015 wild card playoff game that they lost 10-9 to Seattle, but you also cannot forget the 2017 NFC Championship game where we played the Eagles and lost by a whopping score pf 38-7. I have went to many games throughout my life as well, between the Vikings, Twins, Timberwolves and I have even been to a Wild game. Currently I am following the Timberwolves mainly because of how good they have been and I am a super fan for Anthony Edwards. Overall those are just two of the biggest hobbies that I have and I love being able to golf and watch sports all the time!
