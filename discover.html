Why I Chose UMD

I chose UMD because it is close to home and I really like Duluth. I enjoy how we have the great lake and have such a nice community, it will always feel like home. It was also a financially smart choice for me and is helping me with saving a lot of money to hopefully keep me out of debt down the road. I have grown up coming to UMD games my whole life, whether it is hockey, football or basketball I have always been around. I also have grown up coming to high school playoff games here.

Personal Stories

UMD has always been somewhere I thought I would go, my Mother and Uncles have went here and I always thought it was a very nice school. I enjoy watching UMD sports like Hockey, Basketball and on occasion Baseball. I have already met so many people here and it feels such like home. The community here is so great and there is always something going on to make you feel like you arent left out, whether it is a career fair, or a club meeting or some sort of presentation. I have really fallen in love with it here at UMD and I am so glad to call this my home!
